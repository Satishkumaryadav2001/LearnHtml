Alakh Pandey is an Indian entrepreneur and teacher . Alakh is the co-founder and CEO of Physics Valhalla , an Indian educational technology company headquartered in Noida , Uttar Pradesh . [1] [2] [3]

Alakh was born in Allahabad , Uttar Pradesh on October 2, 1991 to Satish Pandey, a private contractor, and Rajat Pandey, a teacher. Alakh received his schooling from Bishop Johnson School, and college from Allahabad and later, he went to study Mechanical Engineering from Harcourt Butler Technical University , Kanpur in 2011. But, he left the university in his third year.

When Alakh was in 11th class, he started giving tuition at Rs 5000 per month in an educational institute in Allahabad. For some time, Alakh continued teaching offline tuition only. In 2016, Alakh started his YouTube channel, Physics Wala and started uploading videos of Physics and Chemistry for classes 9th, 10th, 11th and 12th and All India examinations like JEE, NEET and board examinations of CBSE and ICSE. In 2019 he launched the Physics Valhalla website and mobile application . In 2020, Alakh merged his YouTube channel with Physics Valhalla, an Indian educational technology company and co-founded it with IIT-BHU alumnus Prateek Maheshwari [4] Physics Valhalla is India's sixth edtech unicorn in 2022 It became. [5] [6] As of 2023, his channel has over 10 million subscribers. [7]

A web series, 'Physics Wala', based on the journey of Alakh and PW, was streamed on Amazon Mini TV . [8] [9] He was also seen in a guest role in a popular Indian drama, Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah . (EP-3507) [10]
